About Us

Welcome to our Pokemon website

We are a group of Pokemon enthusiasts who have created this website as a resource for all things Pokemon. Whether you're a collector, a player, or just a fan of the series, we hope you'll find something here that interests you.

Our website is dedicated to providing the most up-to-date information about Pokemon, including lists of all the different Pokemon, their abilities and moves, and much more.

We hope you enjoy using our website as much as we enjoyed creating it.
